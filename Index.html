⚠️ URGENT WARNING: Ongoing scams involving impersonation of celebrity Elon Musk are targeting victims worldwide.

🚨 Elon Musk Impersonation Scam Alert

Criminals are posing as Elon Musk to deceive individuals into sending money.

The FBI has received numerous reports of scammers falsely claiming to be Elon Musk through social media. These scams often promise unrealistic gifts, giveaways, or investment opportunities.

How the Scam Works

1. Initial Contact – Social media or messaging apps
2. Impersonation – Fake Elon Musk profiles
3. The Offer – Crypto doubling / fake investments
4. Payment Request – Crypto, gift cards, wire
5. Disappearance – Scammer vanishes

Warning Signs

❌ Requests for gift card payments
❌ Promises of guaranteed returns or doubled money
❌ Messages claiming to be directly from Elon Musk
❌ Poor grammar or suspicious links
❌ Requests for financial information

Know the Truth

Elon Musk does NOT ask individuals for money directly
No private giveaways via direct messages
No gift card transfers

Reported Losses

Thousands of victims globally
Millions of dollars lost in wire scams
Emotional and financial distress reported

Protect Yourself

Do NOT send money or Gift card
Stop communication immediately
Take screenshots
Report the account
Secure your financial accounts
